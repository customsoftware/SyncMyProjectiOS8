Add a project, task, expense, category or meeting by swiping down from navigation bar over the respective table list.

Update a project's data by swiping down from navigation bar over detail view containing this information. Close the view by tapping outside the view.

To collapse child tasks into the parent task or to expand hidden child tasks, just press on the task until it blinks then release the tap and the sub-tasks will appear, or hide, as the case may be.

Add tasks:
    1.  By swiping down from navigation bar over task list
    2.  Select text in Project notes and tap "Create New Task" menu.
    3.  Select text in Task notes and tap "Create New Task" menu. These tasks will be sub-tasks of the current task.

Where a seach field is visible, swipe up on the list to hide it.

For additional and more indepth help, tap "Info" icon. In iPhone it is in Project list navigation bar. In iPad, it is in Project Notes navigation bar.
